NAME
    KILL CLIENT - Terminates a specific client connection by its numeric ID.

SYNTAX
    KILL CLIENT <client_id>

DESCRIPTION
    Terminates a specific client connection by its numeric ID.

    Use SHOW CLIENTS to find client IDs.

PARAMETERS
    <client_id> - Numeric identifier of the client connection to terminate

EXAMPLES
    Kill a client connection:
        KILL CLIENT 42;

RELATED COMMANDS
    KILL BACKEND
    SHOW
