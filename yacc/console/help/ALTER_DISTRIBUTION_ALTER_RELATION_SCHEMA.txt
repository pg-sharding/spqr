NAME
    ALTER DISTRIBUTION ALTER RELATION SCHEMA - Changes the schema associated
    with an attached relation in the distribution metadata.

SYNTAX
    ALTER DISTRIBUTION <distributionID> ALTER RELATION <relationName>
      SCHEMA <schemaName>

DESCRIPTION
    Changes the schema associated with an attached relation in the distribution
    metadata.

EXAMPLES
    Change relation schema:
        ALTER DISTRIBUTION ds1 ALTER RELATION orders SCHEMA my_schema;
